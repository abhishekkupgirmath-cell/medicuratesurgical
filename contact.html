About company

Medicurate Surgical is a medical technology company specializing in XR-based anatomy education, DICOM-integrated visualization, and simulation-driven medical training. Through partnerships with Virtual Medicine (for advanced anatomy & DICOM software) and SIMLAB Soft (for no-code VR content creation), we provide institutions with a complete ecosystem for modern medical education. We offer: * VR hardware + software bundles (Meta Quest 3/3s/PRO) * Anatomy and surgical visualization in VR with full DICOM support * No-code tools for creating custom VR medical scenarios * High-fidelity manikins and simulation training systems
