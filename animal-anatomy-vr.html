Medicurate Surgical Releases New Title: Animal Anatomy VR

Nov 11, 2025 â€¢ Product Launch

Medicurate Surgical is proud to announce the latest addition to our virtual reality suite: Animal Anatomy VR. Designed for veterinary students, professionals, and enthusiasts, this new title brings the same level of detail/immersion found in our Human Anatomy VR platform to the animal kingdom.

The initial release includes detailed anatomical models for:

  • Canine (Dog)
  • Feline (Cat)
  • Equine (Horse)
  • Bovine (Cow)

"We realized that the veterinary field was underserved in terms of high-quality XR training tools," said the CEO of Medicurate Surgical. "Animal Anatomy VR aims to bridge that gap with photorealistic models and interactive systems."

The application is now available for Institutions and will be coming to the Personal store next month.
