Animal Anatomy VR is an advanced virtual reality platform developed by Virtual Medicine for veterinary students, professionals, and educational institutions. It provides detailed anatomical visualization and interactive learning experiences for veterinary medicine education.

The Virtual Medicine platform includes detailed anatomical models for:

  • Canine (Dog)
  • Feline (Cat)
  • Equine (Horse)
  • Bovine (Cow)

Animal Anatomy VR, developed by Virtual Medicine, offers high-quality XR-based training with photorealistic models and interactive systems for comprehensive veterinary anatomical study. Medicurate provides hardware integration and support services for institutional deployments.
