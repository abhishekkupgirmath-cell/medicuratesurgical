Innovating the Education of Healthcare Professionals and Students

Virtual Medicine aims to revolutionize medical education with advanced technology. Our mission is to enhance how future doctors are trained by providing innovative tools for learning human anatomy.

Human Anatomy VR is our flagship product, offering an advanced medical anatomy platform. This award-winning tool utilizes VR, MR, and AR to create a comprehensive 3D anatomical atlas with a 3D VR DICOM viewer.

Designed for mobile devices, tablets, and VR headsets, it replaces traditional dissection with an immersive experience, improving understanding and performance. Studies show a 45% improvement in student results and 92% reporting enhanced learning.

Trusted by institutions in over 180 countries, Human Anatomy VR supports students, professionals, and educational institutions with over 13,000 realistic anatomical structures and features like bone mapping and multi-user collaboration.

Full Package of intuitive and useful features.

The intuitive design of Human Anatomy VR anatomical atlas enables easy use by anyone.
No previous experience with virtual reality is needed.

Easy Manipulations
Navigate anatomical models with simple controls or hand tracking for a more immersive experience. Move, rotate, and interact naturally using just your hands.
VR DICOM
The 3D VR DICOM viewer converts CT and MRI scans into 3D models for exploring anatomy and pathology. Perfect for education, pre-op planning, and simulation centers.
3D Cadavers
Explore over 1,500 stereoscopic cadaver images in 3D. This feature offers a lifelike and immersive experience for detailed and realistic anatomical study.
Body Actions
The Body Actions feature includes over 500 body movements, offering an in-depth exploration of muscle functions in dynamic 3D detail.
Microanatomy Models
Study detailed 3D microanatomy models to explore complex cell structures, giving you a deeper understanding of the body at the microscopic level.
Multi-User
Multi-User feature enables collaborative learning, allowing real-time exploration and discussion of anatomical structures in Virtual or Mixed reality.

VR DICOM

The 3D VR DICOM viewer allows users to view CT and MRI scans in the virtual environment of the Human Anatomy VR application. By converting 2D DICOM images into a detailed interactive 3D model, it offers an immersive way to explore anatomical structures in virtual or mixed reality. This tool is ideal for simulation centers, medical education, and pre-surgical planning, providing a realistic and interactive method for analyzing both anatomy and pathology in real time.

Multi-user
collaboration

Our multi-user function allows you to connect up to 100 people in a single VR room to present a lecture. Each connected student and teacher has a 3D Avatar, which they can personalize. Connected students can be seen and heard as if they were together in the same room – regardless of where in the world they are.

Test Manager

Test Manager allows lecturers to create and evaluate immersive tests directly from their laptops or tablets using the Virtual Medicine Portal. Create tests through a web browser, and students can take them in their VR headsets, transforming the learning experience. Afterward, lecturers can review results either in VR or on the portal, ensuring flexibility and efficiency.

The Virtual Medicine Portal also simplifies license management. Easily purchase, update, and track license validity with renewal reminders, keeping your educational resources always up to date.

Medical Education and Training
Human Anatomy VR is revolutionizing medical education by supplementing traditional teaching methods in medical schools and universities. Our detailed 3D models and animations provide a comprehensive understanding of anatomical structures and spatial relationships. While viewing these structures enhances knowledge, the true value lies in the interactive features. Remarkably, 92% of surveyed students reported that our platform significantly improved their learning and comprehension of anatomy.
Collaborative Learning
Educational institutions use the Multi-User feature of Human Anatomy VR for in-class and remote teaching sessions, enabling instructors and students to collaborate in real time. This feature allows participants to explore anatomical structures together in an interactive virtual classroom, providing an engaging learning experience. This feature is advantageous for distance learning and external programs, as it ensures that students access high-quality education regardless of their location.
Patient Consultation
Medical professionals utilize Human Anatomy VR to effectively communicate with patients about their medical conditions and upcoming procedures. By visualizing their condition in Virtual or Mixed Reality, patients gain a clearer and more comprehensive understanding of their health issues and the proposed treatment plans. This is particularly helpful with physical therapy and rehabilitation. This immersive experience demystifies complex medical information and empowers patients to make well-informed decisions regarding their care.
Professional Development
Human Anatomy VR is transforming how hospitals and medical institutions train and develop their staff. By using 3D models and interactive features, our platform helps healthcare professionals to gain a deeper understanding of complex anatomical concepts. They can work with lifelike 3D models and scenarios, boosting their theoretical knowledge in a safe, risk-free setting. This hands-on approach makes learning more engaging while ensuring that medical staff is better prepared for real-life situations.
